Our core value is independence

Blomy’s mission is to help consumers make better choices for their health.
In doing so, it aims to drive manufacturers to offer better products.

No ads
Blomy is a completely ad-free app. Brands cannot pay Blomy to advertise their products.
No influence
Scores and recommendations are obtained independently, with absolutely no influence from outside brands or manufacturers.
Data protection
Blomy does not process or sell user data. All user data remains strictly confidential.
Responsible funding
Our 3 sources of revenue
The paid version of the app: users can subscribe to the Member version, which comes with a number of additional features, such as a search bar, offline mode, and custom alerts notifying users of the presence of palm oil, gluten, or lactose. This premium version is extremely important to Blomy, as it helps maintain the project’s independence.
A calendar of seasonal fruits and vegetables (available in France only). This adorable calendar can be placed on a counter or hung on your kitchen wall. It's also socially responsible, made in France on paper derived from sustainably managed forests and packaged by individuals from a work reintegration program. Incidentally, we donate 1% of the revenue to environmental organizations.
The Nutrition Program (in French only): an online program allowing participants to learn the basics of healthy nutrition in 10 weeks. During the program, you'll learn the 10 Golden Rules of healthy eating and gain access to 50 recipes based on those rules. A nutritionist is on hand to answer all your questions and provide personalized suggestions.
A committed team
Behind Blomy is a small team of 11 enthusiastic individuals who want to help others and be part of making a difference.
